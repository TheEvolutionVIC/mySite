Overspending is a problem

Established in 2019, after being overworked, stressed, depressed, tired, I decided to do something about it. We’ve all been there!

Hey folks, this is Jalal, I worked 2 even 3 casual jobs at once, just to make up for all the overspending, every week, after completing long shifts, I feel entitled to treat myself for all the hard work, I still believe I am, difference is, how much of that money I can afford to spend if I wanted to get out of the pay-check to paycheck lifestyle.

Overworking is a problem

Every dollar I’ve overspent was a 3 mintues of extra work I must put in the next week, in the heat of the moment carelessly choosing to work these extra 3 minutes, after all the overspending I’d come to realise that I have added at least 10-20 hours on top of the 20 hour target that I had for myself.

So, the following week, I’d overwork myself, up to 50+ hours on average, and then have the same feeling, exhausted, did a great job, “I deserve to reward myself”. Vicious cycle!

EVolt’s idea came by! Goodbye to financial stress and anxiety.

Welcome Abundance

After me and some of my friends tested the system behind EVolt, we noticed a dramatic change in our spending and working habits, we felt abundant, in control of how we spend our waking hours, it was all uphell from there on forward. We decided to share that feeling with every student feeling stressed about their financial life.
